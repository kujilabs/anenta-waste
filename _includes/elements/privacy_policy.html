Data collection

The following data is collected from all visitors for analytics purposes: anonymized IP address, browser details, pages visited within the site; and is retained forever. No personally-identifiable information is collected unless you consent.

If you subscribe to my newsletter (through any form on this website, including forms for downloading freebies), the following data will be collected and retained:

  • Email address and the name that you enter into the form; as well as the IP address and the page URL that you signed up from;
  • A list of pages you visited on this website (including the pages you visited on this website before signing up. Note: before you sign up, the list is only kept in your browser and is not uploaded anywhere until after you confirmed your registration.);
  • Which automated emails you opened and interacted with;
  • Whether or not you have purchased any products from me.

This information is used to send out newsletters and to personalize the website experience for you.

This information is retained forever. Unsubscribing from newsletter stops further collection but does not delete any data collected before.

You must be at least 16 years old to sign up on this website.

Sharing of Data

Your personal data is not shared with third parties except as listed below.

We currently use:

  • Google Analytics: no personally-identifiable information is shared;
  • Drip: your name, email, IP address, list of pages you visited and emails you opened/clicked.
  • FastSpring: your name, email, order history.

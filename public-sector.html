Public Sector

Offering an end-to-end waste management service and working with your existing contracts or specifying and procuring them from scratch - we deliver value for money. Our online operational waste management platform drives the efficiencies, service quality and accountability that the public sector requires.

Anenta are proud to support the public sector. Serving both NHS England and local authorities, we have achieved significant financial savings and have innovated to improve services.

We understand the challenges faced by public sector organisations and the difficulties faced as a result of changing governmental strategy. We are well versed in assisting our public sector partners in delivering their strategic targets.

We drive efficiencies and innovation through our online management platform. Designed and developed in-house it enables rapid deployment of new services, communication to audiences large or small, financial control and overall operational management.
